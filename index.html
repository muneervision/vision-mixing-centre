About the Studio

Fifteen years behind
the lens, in front of the moment.

Vision Mixing Centre is a Srinagar-based wedding photography and film studio built around one idea: your day should be documented the way it actually felt, not the way a template says it should look.

Our team shoots with high-end cinema and full-frame equipment, edits in-house, and treats every album and film as a one-off production rather than a package on a shelf.
