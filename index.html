About the Studio

Fifteen years of Kashmiri weddings, told in light

Vision Mixing Centre is a full-service wedding photography and cinematic film studio founded in Srinagar. Our team works with high-end cinema cameras, drones and lighting to document weddings, engagements and portraits across the valley.

Led by Founder & Creative Director Muneer Mattoo, we've built our reputation one wedding at a time — combining traditional Kashmiri ceremony coverage with a modern, cinematic visual language.
